Rhythm of Bamboos

The Rhythm of Bamboos is a sustainable design project.

We intend to use the environmentally-friendly material bamboos to build public spaces for women and children in rural villages in China to compensate for the inequality caused by patriarchy and urbanization. Project-Kits will be ready once the Rhythm launches successfully.

Sustainability and Equality for all!

Designers: Xiaojing Li & Jiaona Hu

Funded by: Prince Claus Fund & Goethe Institute
